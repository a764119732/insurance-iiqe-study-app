## v3 Rules Applied

- `simple_explanation` must be simplified Chinese.
- No source-reference wording such as "原始解析", "原文指出", "根据教材", "教材第几章第几节", or section codes such as "1.1.2a".
- Strict 4 sections only:
  1. 考点
  2. 结论 + 解释
  3. 一一解释其他选项为什么错
  4. 记忆口诀
- No section 5.
- Every wrong option must have a concrete reason.
- Combination questions must explain i / ii / iii / iv first, then explain A/B/C/D combinations.
- Reverse or exception questions must state the question direction.
- Generic comparison phrases are forbidden.
